Learning experiences that make technology feel accessible and inspiring.

Our programs help learners discover programming, digital creativity, and applied technology through hands-on projects and supportive guidance.

Workshops

Short, interactive sessions for classrooms, clubs, and community groups focused on coding fundamentals and creative tech.

Courses

Structured learning paths with clear milestones for students building skills over time.

Resources

How it works

Practice technology with guided, creative experiences.

Every Lumina Learn experience is designed to be hands-on, supportive, and relevant to learners’ interests. Students build actual projects while gaining confidence with code and digital tools.

1

Start with an idea, choose a guided activity, and discover how technology can bring it to life.

2

Follow clear lessons, experiment with projects, and learn by making.

3

Share work, reflect on what was learned, and build the next project with new skills.
