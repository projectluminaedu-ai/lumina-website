About Lumina

We believe technology can illuminate possibilities and create meaningful change.

Lumina is a student-led nonprofit made up of developers who create practical technology to help people and communities.

Who we are

Lumina is a community of student developers who build technology with purpose.

Our mission

To empower students to use technology for good by building accessible tools, solving real-world problems, and creating impact through software development.

Our vision

A future where students everywhere have the opportunity to create with technology and make a difference in their communities.

What we value

We value collaboration, learning, accessibility, and building software that helps real people.

Our approach

We focus on projects that are useful, thoughtful, and built for real-world impact.

Lumina is not a personal portfolio or startup. It is a nonprofit community where students collaborate, learn, and build technology that matters.

“Technology can illuminate possibilities, reveal solutions, and empower people.”
