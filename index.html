Our approach

Two pillars working together to expand access to technology.

Lumina Learn

Workshops, courses, and resources that help students and communities understand programming, digital skills, and creative technology.

Lumina Tools

Helpful apps and interactive tools that let people learn, create, and solve problems with technology.

Shared philosophy

Every project follows the same goal: use technology to empower people through learning and practical tools.
